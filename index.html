About Me

Hello!

I am currently in my fourth year of study at The University of British Columbia studying Computer Science. I took a gap year after Highschool and lived in Kyoto, Japan for one year. I lived nearby the most popular tourist stop in the world Fushimi Inari. I'm a huge supporter of people taking time off after highschool. I felt it gave me time to figure out who I was and what I wanted.

My main interests of study are computer graphics, game development, and game engine creation. My favorite games are Dark Souls, Persona 5, Skyrim, Portal 2, Undertale, and Vanilla World of Warcraft.


I got into programming in Grade 6 when my friends and I wanted to create a MapleStory private server. My earliest published work is a script for an NPC which can be found here. Please excuse any grammar, spelling errors or bad jokes, I was 10 years old.

After MapleStory I got super into Minecraft and learned how to create mods. My first mod ever being the amazing "Rainbow Smoothie Mod". You should definitely check it out. In addition to mods, I also ran one of the largest "Dwarfs Vs. Zombies" Minecraft servers. During its peak we would sometimes have 500 players online! Popular Youtuber "TheCampingRusher" published a few videos of himself playing on my server DwarfsCraft: Check them out!
