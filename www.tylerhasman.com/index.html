About Me

I am currently in my fourth year of study at The University of British Columbia in Vancouver, BC, Canada. My main interests are computer graphics, game development and programming languages. Specifically how we can design languages to better support creating games on top of them.


I have experience working with Java, C/C++, C#, and Racket. I have also worked on open source projects, such as Tome and LibGDX. Part of my passion lies in creating tools that can assist programmers in closer working with designers to produce new games.
